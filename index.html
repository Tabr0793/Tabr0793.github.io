HUMAN CENTERED SOLUTIONS.

A lifelong visual learner with a fervor for creative & well-delivered design, Taylor completed her B. ENVD with an emphasis in Architecture at the University of Colorado, Boulder. After graduating in 2015, her curious spirit took her from her Chicago roots to the West Coast, where she began her architecture career, in San Jose, CA. It wasn’t long before Taylor realized her entrepreneurial spirit needed something more - something faster, more colorful - a career that could help others achieve their dreams, too.

Taylor was introduced to UI/UX design by some tech savvy friends; it instantly struck the perfect chord in her creative, order-loving mind. Achieving beautiful solutions for internet pioneers is Taylor’s jam. Leave your branding, user experience & marketing needs to her.

Taylor holds a certificate in UI/UX design from BLOC.
